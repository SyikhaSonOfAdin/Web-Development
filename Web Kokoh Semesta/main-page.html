Kokoh Semesta Products & Services

Profile

Started in the year 1981 as a construction company for site fabrication and erection of storage tanks, building structures and related civil works, we have looked forward for the necessity for own works, wherein we can control quality and time schedule better. We also felt the need to support our government to go through its industrialization programs with self sufficiency. Thus was born PT. Kokoh Semesta with its 37.000 sq.m working space, located near Tanjung Priok seaport. With more than 24 cranes to facilitate rigging, we are forged to build heavy equipment, pressure vessel, heat exchangers, build-up beams for multy story complex steel trusses, steel towers etc. Her, we strictly train our personnel to stand up to the quality and time schedule as required by client. Though basically we thought of catering to the inland requirements, through our hard working, quality consciousness, and fair dealing, we have already spread our wing outside Indonesia.

Vision

Being world-class company in steel construstion

Mission

To serve the need of industry, housing and agriculture in manufacturing high quality of steel construction in timely manner and low price.
